So if you are someone who has the vigour to learn but does not have the means to afford it, then this program is for you.

Pick My Career by LMES.org

LMES has enabled thorough understanding of science and engineering concepts through its YouTube channel using real world applications. We have reached over 1+ million people through social media and lakhs of students through our BigBang science events and Science Workshops.

We understood that there is a strong need to penetrate into the rural, government schools and enable the underprivileged kids to have the similar knowledge that any other student could receive from us. Hence LMES Academy Pvt Ltd started an EduTech, 12A certified NGO “LMES Foundation” with the above said mission in 2014.
